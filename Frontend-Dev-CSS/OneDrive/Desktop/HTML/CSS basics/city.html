About the City

Vibrant streets, riverside parks, and a rich cultural history define this city. The waterfront promenades are ideal for walks and local vendors offer seasonal foods.

Culture & Events

Annual festivals celebrate music, food, and crafts. Museums and galleries host rotating exhibitions. Theaters offer local and international plays.

Places to Visit

Parks, historic quarters, and markets provide a full experience. Long promenades with scenic views are perfect for photography and leisure.
